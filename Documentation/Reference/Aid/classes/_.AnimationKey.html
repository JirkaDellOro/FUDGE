Class AnimationKey

Holds information about set points in time, their accompanying values as well as their slopes. Also holds a reference to the AnimationFunctions that come in and out of the sides. The AnimationFunctions are handled by the AnimationSequences. Saved inside an AnimationSequence.

Lukas Scheuerle, HFU, 2019
