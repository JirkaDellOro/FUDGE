Class PhysicsQuaternion

Storing and manipulating rotations in the form of quaternions. Constructed out of the 4 components x,y,z,w. Commonly used to calculate rotations in physics engines. Class mostly used internally to bridge the in FUDGE commonly used angles in degree to OimoPhysics quaternion system.

authors

Marko Fehrenbach, HFU, 2020
